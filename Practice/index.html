About Me

I’m Zeraki-Tobias “Brockton” Stover, a data analyst and computational physicist with experience in Python, C++, SQL, and scientific data pipelines. I’ve built tools for satellite telemetry analysis at SETI, contributed to radio astronomy signal-processing work at UC Berkeley, and developed simulation and visualization software for computational physics research. My work sits at the intersection of software engineering, data analysis, and applied science, and I’m focused on building reliable, high-impact technical solutions.
